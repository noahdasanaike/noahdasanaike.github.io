I'm a third-year PhD candidate and the Carl J. Friedrich Fellow in the Department of Government at Harvard University.

I’m primarily interested in structuralist approaches to political behavior and institutions, with a focus on their geographical (spatial/place and natural) origins. I'm also interested in large-scale measurement and computation.
